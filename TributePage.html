Mahatma Gandhi

1869 - 1948

About Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, also known as Mahatma Gandhi, was the leader of the Indian independence movement against British rule. Employing nonviolent civil disobedience, Gandhi led India to independence and inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

He is unofficially called the Father of the Nation in India. His principles of truth (Satya) and nonviolence (Ahimsa) are admired worldwide.
